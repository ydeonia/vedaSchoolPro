Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I cancel?
You keep full access to your data. Export everything before cancellation. We never delete data without explicit request. After cancellation, data is retained for 90 days.
Is auto-save enabled?
Yes — every form submission is saved immediately. For attendance, marks, and fee collection, data is saved the moment you click submit. There's no "Save" button you might forget.
How often are backups taken?
Depends on your plan: Free (weekly), Starter (weekly), Professional (daily), Enterprise (real-time). Backups are retained for 30-365 days based on plan.
Can someone tamper with fee receipts?
No. All PDF documents carry a "VedaSchoolPro Verified" watermark, are generated server-side, and linked to audit logs. Any modification attempt would be detected.
Who can see my child's data?
Only you (as parent), your child (as student), the school admin, and their class teacher. No other parents, students, or staff can access your child's records.
Can I recover deleted data?
Yes — most deletes are "soft deletes" (data is marked inactive, not removed). Backups provide an additional safety net. Contact support for recovery.
